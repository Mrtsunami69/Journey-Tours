Travel Documentation

Proper documentation is essential for a smooth family trip. Here are the key items to prepare:

  • Passports – Must be valid for at least 6 months beyond your return date for all family members
  • Visas – Check if your destination requires visas (we can help with applications)
  • Vaccinations – Ensure routine vaccines are up to date; some countries require specific shots (e.g., yellow fever)
  • Birth Certificates – Required for children under 18 when traveling internationally
  • Travel Authorization Letter – Needed if one parent is traveling alone with children
  • Other – Copies of insurance, itinerary, and emergency contacts

Always verify requirements at travel.gc.ca or the destination country's official site. We can assist with gathering and checking documents.
