About Us

JourneyWell Tours is a family-focused travel agency based in Hamilton, Ontario. We specialize in creating affordable, safe, and well-planned vacations for everyday families middle-income households who travel during school holidays and want stress-free experiences without luxury prices.

Our team has years of experience understanding what families really need: convenience, value, safety, and peace of mind. We bundle flights, hotels, cars, activities, and extras so parents can relax and enjoy time with their kids.

Our employees are registered with the Travel Industry Council of Ontario (TICO) and many hold the Certified Travel Counsellor (CTC) designation. This ensures professional expertise, ethical standards, and consumer protection. We are also members of industry associations like Travel Leaders Network for access to preferred rates, training, and marketing support.
