About Us

JourneyWell Tours is a family-focused travel agency based in Hamilton, Ontario, with over 10 years of experience in planning safe, affordable, and memorable vacations. Our agency specializes in helping middle-income families enjoy well-organized travel experiences without the stress of complicated planning.

Years in Business: Over 10 years of experience serving families across Canada.

Planning travel for an entire family can often be overwhelming. Parents must coordinate flights, accommodations, activities, and travel documents for multiple travellers while ensuring destinations are safe and enjoyable for children. At JourneyWell Tours, our goal is to simplify this process by providing expert travel guidance and personalized planning services designed specifically for families.

Our experienced and certified travel advisors work with trusted travel suppliers around the world to create carefully curated vacation experiences. We assist families with selecting destinations, organizing travel packages, and recommending family-friendly resorts, cruises, and attractions that balance relaxation for parents with exciting activities for children.

We have successfully helped hundreds of families plan stress-free vacations tailored to their needs and budgets, building long-term trust and satisfaction with our clients.

Our employees are registered with the Travel Industry Council of Ontario (TICO), and many advisors hold the Certified Travel Counsellor (CTC) designation. These certifications demonstrate our commitment to professional expertise, ethical standards, and consumer protection within the travel industry. We are also members of organizations such as Travel Leaders Network, providing access to exclusive partnerships, training, and travel resources.

Our Mission:
To make family travel easy, safe, and memorable by combining professional expertise with personalized customer support.
