Travel Insurance

Travel insurance provides financial protection and peace of mind for families traveling abroad. JourneyWell Tours helps clients understand and select the right coverage to protect against unexpected situations such as medical emergencies, trip cancellations, and travel disruptions.

Emergency Medical Insurance

Covers unexpected medical expenses while traveling, including hospital visits, emergency treatment, and medical evacuation if required.

  • Hospital and doctor fees
  • Emergency medical transport
  • 24/7 assistance services

Trip Cancellation & Interruption

Protects your investment if your trip is cancelled or interrupted due to unexpected events such as illness, emergencies, or travel restrictions.

  • Pre-paid trip cost protection
  • Coverage for cancellations
  • Reimbursement for interruptions

Baggage & Personal Belongings

Covers lost, stolen, or damaged baggage and personal items during your trip, ensuring you are compensated for unexpected losses.

  • Lost luggage coverage
  • Delayed baggage support
  • Personal item protection

Travel Delay & Disruption

Provides compensation for delays caused by weather, airline issues, or unexpected disruptions that affect your travel schedule.

  • Flight delay coverage
  • Accommodation during delays
  • Meal and transport expenses

Insurance Consultation

Our advisors help families choose the most suitable insurance plans based on destination, budget, and travel needs to ensure complete protection.

  • Policy comparison
  • Coverage explanation
  • Personalized recommendations
