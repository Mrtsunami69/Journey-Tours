Travel Insurance

Travel insurance protects your family from unexpected events such as medical emergencies, trip cancellation or interruption, lost or delayed baggage, flight delays, or accidents. It's especially important when traveling with children, as it provides peace of mind and financial security.

We recommend purchasing insurance for every trip. Our sample plan includes:

  • Family Shield Plan – $99 per trip (up to $5 million medical coverage, trip cancellation up to $10,000, baggage loss/delay, 24/7 emergency assistance)
  • Coverage for pre-existing conditions (with waiver if purchased early)
  • Child-inclusive policies (no extra cost for kids under 18)
